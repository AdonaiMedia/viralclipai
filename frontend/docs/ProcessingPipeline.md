# Processing Pipeline

Responsible for processing uploaded videos from start to finish.

---

## Pipeline Stages

1. Upload
2. Inspection
3. Audio Extraction
4. Transcription
5. AI Analysis
6. Clip Detection
7. Thumbnail Generation
8. Database Save
9. Export

---

## Processing Flow

Video Upload

↓

Video Inspection

↓

Extract Audio

↓

Generate Transcript

↓

AI Analysis

↓

Detect Viral Moments

↓

Generate Clips

↓

Generate Thumbnail

↓

Save Results

↓

Ready For Export

---

## Output

- Transcript
- AI Analysis
- Viral Score
- Video Clips
- Thumbnail
- Social Content
